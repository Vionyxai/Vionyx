3. Why We Collect It

All data collected has one purpose: providing you with the VIONYX service. Specifically:

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session.
  • To store your journal entries so they persist across sessions.
  • To build a compressed pattern context from your history, which is injected into the AI's session to enable pattern recognition over time.
  • To improve the platform based on aggregate, non-identifying usage patterns.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not use your data to train AI models.

4. Sensitive Personal Information

Under the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), information that reveals your mental or emotional health, psychological state, or behavioral patterns is classified as sensitive personal information. The content you share in VIONYX — including emotional triggers, unfiltered expressions, and pattern reflections — falls within this category.

We use sensitive personal information solely to provide the VIONYX service to you. We do not sell it, share it for advertising, or use it for any purpose beyond what you explicitly use the platform for.

You have the right to direct us to limit the use and disclosure of your sensitive personal information to what is necessary to perform the service. To exercise this right, contact us at vionyxai555@gmail.com.

5. How It Is Stored

Supabase — Your account data and journal entries are stored in Supabase, a cloud database provider acting as our service provider. Supabase encrypts data at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher. Your data is stored in a dedicated, access-controlled database table. Supabase processes your data only as directed by VIONYX and under a data processing agreement.

Anthropic Claude API — AI responses are generated through the Anthropic Claude API, routed through a Cloudflare Worker proxy operated by VIONYX. Messages sent to the AI are processed to generate responses. Anthropic does not store or train on API inputs under their API usage policies. Your conversation content is not retained by Anthropic or Cloudflare after processing.

Browser sessionStorage — Conversation history within an active session is temporarily stored in your browser's sessionStorage. This data is scoped to your tab and is automatically cleared when you close the tab or sign out. It never leaves your device.

6. Service Providers

We share personal information with the following service providers solely to operate the platform. These companies are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes:

  • Supabase — database storage and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI response generation via API (no retention post-processing)
  • Cloudflare — API proxy routing
  • Vercel — application hosting and delivery

We do not sell your data to any of these providers or any other party. We do not share your individual data with other VIONYX users.
